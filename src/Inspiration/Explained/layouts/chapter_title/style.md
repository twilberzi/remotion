# Layout: Chapter Title

## Purpose
Use this layout to introduce a new section or chapter in an explainer or educational video.
This slide should create a clear mental reset and signal: “new topic starting.”

## Structure
- Centered chapter label pill at top (ex: “Chapter 2”)
- Large main headline centered on screen
- No supporting graphics or icons
- Full-screen layout

## Visual Style
- Background: Soft light gradient or subtle motion background (from brand/elements/backgrounds)
- Chapter label:
  - Rounded pill shape
  - Brand accent color (red/pink gradient)
  - White text
- Headline text:
  - Dark brand text color
  - Large, bold, high-contrast
  - Max 2 lines
  - Center aligned
- Plenty of negative space

## Motion Rules
- Background: slow subtle motion (light gradient or noise loop)
- Chapter pill fades in first
- Headline fades/slides up second
- No icons or diagrams
- No complex motion

## Timing Guidelines
- Total duration: 1.5–2.5 seconds
- Chapter label appears first (0.3–0.5s)
- Headline appears shortly after (0.5–0.8s)
- Hold on screen for readability

## When to Use
- Chapter transitions
- Topic changes
- Section breaks in YouTube videos
- Course-style modules
- Explainer series structure

## Do Not
- Do not add icons
- Do not add diagrams
- Do not clutter with extra text
- Do not animate too aggressively
